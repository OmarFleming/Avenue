Frequently Asked Questions

What is Avenue?

Avenue is a tool designed 'by property investors for property investors'. Our mission is simple: to make owning property as easy as owning any other investment asset class. Think of it as the control centre for your property portfolio. It allows you to easily organise and manage your properties in one beautiful dashboard and optimise your portfolio for future growth.

Is Avenue right for me?

Whether you are novice investor having purchased your first investment property, or a sophisticated investor with a portfolio of properties. Avenue can help you. We are also adding more features to help make managing properties a breeze, whether it is 1 property or 100 properties.

How much does it cost?

For now, it is completely free.

Is my data safe?

Yes. All your information is encrypted using industry leading encryption methods. Also, we would never share any of your information to 3rd parties.
